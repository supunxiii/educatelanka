About Us

Our mission is to empower schools in Sri Lanka with the tools and technology they need to provide an enriching and personalized learning experience.
With EducateLanka, schools can harness the power of modern technology to create a dynamic and interactive learning environment. Our customized LMS solutions are crafted to meet the specific needs of each educational institution, ensuring seamless integration into their existing curriculum and teaching methods. Science Day

Berkshire High School is excited to celebrate Science Day, a special occasion dedicated to nurturing young scientific minds. On this day, our school community comes together to showcase the incredible achievements and talents of our budding scientists. Through engaging experiments, informative presentations, and interactive activities, we aim to foster a deep love and appreciation for science among our students.

Colours Eve

Join us in celebrating the outstanding achievements of our talented athletes at Berkshire High School's 'Colours Eve for Sports Excellence'! This special event is dedicated to honoring the dedication and hard work of our exceptional sportswomen who have brought pride and glory to our school through their remarkable feats in various sports.

Interhouse Sports Meet

Get ready for a day filled with spirited competition and camaraderie at Berkshire High School's Interhouse Sports Meet! This exciting event brings together our school's diverse houses in a friendly battle of athleticism and sportsmanship. Students from all houses will showcase their talent and determination as they compete in a variety of sporting events, from sprints to relays, and everything in between
